# Monkhub Website — AI-First Repositioning Blueprint

A complete rethink of monkhub.com: new positioning, new narrative, new information
architecture, new design language, and **paste-ready copy** — engineered so the rebuild
**ships more leads without breaking existing SEO**.

> **The one-line thesis:** Monkhub is no longer a generic "we build apps / blockchain /
> AR-VR" shop. It is an **AI-first product engineering studio that takes AI agents from
> pilot to production** — with Voice AI, Geospatial Intelligence, Mobile, XR, and Gaming
> as the pillars that AI runs through.

---

## Why the current site underperforms (from the page-by-page audit)

- Generic, undifferentiated "service-name-swapped" boilerplate across 12 service pages.
- Placeholder **"0+" / "100% Project Delivered"** stats that read as broken/unfinished.
- **5 dead "soft-404" portfolio pages** served as live URLs (Mnee, gamego, solarplace, gamodify, easynft).
- **Image-only case studies** with **empty `<h1>`s** and `alt=""` — invisible to Google and AI crawlers.
- **No H1 on the homepage.** Typos on key pages ("WEB 3.O", "ETHERIUM", "Industy Experts", footer "Web 3.O").
- **3 redundant lead pages** (free-quote / get-free-estimation / get-free-consultation) cannibalizing each other.
- A careers page with **zero jobs**, a privacy policy **stale since 2021**, no team/leadership, no live demos.

None of these are fatal — the underlying Next.js build is fast and server-rendered. They're
all fixable, and fixing them while repositioning is the opportunity.

## How to use it

1. **Read `01` and `02` first** — positioning + IA are the spine everything hangs off.
2. Hand `03`–`06` to whoever writes/builds pages — the copy is paste-ready; bracketed
   `[LIKE THIS]` markers are **the only things you must fill with real data** (real client
   names, real metrics). **Do not ship a single bracket or a single "0+".**
3. Hand `07` to design/front-end and `08` to whoever owns the deploy + Search Console.
4. **Inviolable rule:** you can change design, copy, IA, and positioning as aggressively
   as you want — but **every old URL must 301 to its new home, and surviving pages must keep
   their title/H1 keyword + content depth.** That's the line between "bold redesign" and
   "lost our rankings." Details in `08`.

## The strategic bet in one paragraph

Every agency now says "we do AI." The buyers have heard it and stopped believing it. The
firms winning AI work in 2026 (Sierra, Thoughtworks, Cognition, Scale, Decagon) win by being
**specific, outcome-led, and anti-hype**: they name the buyer's pain, attach a hard number to
every claim, demote the technology to proof, and lead with **"we get this to production
reliably"** — because ~67% of AI projects die in the pilot and everyone knows it. Monkhub's
edge is that it doesn't just build agents in a vacuum — it ships them **into real products and
real environments**: into mobile apps, into voice channels, onto maps and satellite imagery,
into immersive XR. That breadth, told as *one coherent AI-first story* instead of 12 disconnected
service pages, is the repositioning. The website's job is to make a serious buyer feel, in the
first five seconds, that this is the team that ships.
